KONASEEMA

Konaseema is a beautiful region in Andhra Pradesh known for its natural beauty. It is located in the delta of the Godavari River. Konaseema is often called the “Kerala of Andhra Pradesh.” It is famous for its lush green fields and coconut trees. The canals of the Godavari River add more beauty to the region. The area is known for its peaceful environment and traditional lifestyle. Konaseema has many temples and celebrates festivals with great enthusiasm. It is a great tourist destination for relaxation.
